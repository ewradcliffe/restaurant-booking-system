Welcome to The Blue Boar Inn.

Welcome to The Blue Boar Inn, a traditional restaurant nestled deep in the heart of the English countryside.

Records show an inn on this site since at least the 14th century. It is believed our name may well have been inspired by the personal heraldy of the Earls of Oxford and Henry VII may well have stayed here on the way to his coronation. The present building dates from the 17th Century after the original was destroyed in the civil war.

We offer a selection of hearty, traditional dishes with a contemporary twist. Our chefs have experience at some of the country's most prestigious establishments and we work directly with local producers to source the very best ingredients, so you can always be assured your meal will be of the very highest standard.

With roaring fires and a relaxed atmosphere we are perfect after a day exploring the countryside. Four legged friends are welcome.
